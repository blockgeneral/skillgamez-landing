STICKMAN FIGHT
Read your opponent. Strike first. One round, 60 seconds, KO or best HP wins.
📱 Touch: left thumb = joystick (push up to jump, down to crouch) · 👊 tap = high punch, HOLD = BAT SMASH · 🦶 low kick · 🛡 hold to block (with crouch = low guard) · 💨 dodge · ⚡ hold & release = charged slam
Rules: HIGH beats standing · LOW beats crouchers but is slower · CROUCH ducks highs (and can throw LOW pokes) · JUMP sails over lows and over your rival · hold BLOCK to absorb · crouch+block (any order) = low guard: highs whiff over you · hold ⚡ CHARGE and release: huge damage, smashes through low guard — but miss it and you are stunned a full second · SMASH: overhead bat — crushes straight through a standing block and catches jumpers (low guard slips under it; strikes beat its windup) · DODGE backsteps · jump over your opponent to switch sides — but airborne you can be hit by highs and bats
Keys: A/D or ←→ move · W/↑ jump · S/↓ crouch (hold) · J high · K low · U smash · Space block (hold) · I charge (hold) · L dodge
 Stamina runs everything — swing wild or hide in block and you will be EXHAUSTED: 1 second stunned, free punish, 40 damage.
